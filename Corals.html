Ocean Acidification and Coral

Ever heard of Coral? Nemo's mother? You've heard enough about fish so how about Coral, that tough, rough, and sort've scuffed stuff on the bottom of the ocean that you might occasionally see on a walk on a beach somewhere in hawaii? That's coral. That stuff gets bleached by the increasing acidity of the ocean. That's not good for the ocean and its enviroments. In turn not good for us.
